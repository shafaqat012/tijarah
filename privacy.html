TIJARAH – PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: September 2025 | Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tijarah, a local marketplace platform connecting buyers and sellers in your area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app.

By using Tijarah, you agree to this policy, which complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Google Account Data: Email address and name for sign-in
  • Profile Information: Username, ratings, and reviews
  • Contact Information: Email address for account management
  • GPS Location Data: Used only during app use for location-based listings
  • Phone Number: Optional for buyer-seller communication

2.2 Content You Create

  • Listings, messages, reviews, and search data

2.3 Device & Technical Information

  • Device model, OS version, crash logs, usage analytics, and permissions (camera, storage, notifications)

2.4 Automatically Collected Data

  • Ad interaction, analytics, Google Advertising ID (GAID), and rate-limiting data

3. What Information Is Public

Publicly Visible: Username, profile photo, listings, and reviews.

Private: Email, precise location, chat messages, and device details.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Enable authentication and core features
  • Provide secure, location-based listings
  • Improve app experience and detect abuse
  • Deliver messages, notifications, and updates
  • Show relevant ads (via AdMob)

5. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services like Firebase, Google AdMob, and Play Services. Data is shared only to enable core features—never sold. Legal disclosures may occur when required by law.

6. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure Google Cloud storage
  • Firestore security rules and admin moderation
  • Regular security audits and app updates

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Data retained only while your account is active
  • Listings auto-delete after 7 days of expiry
  • Chats are auto-deleted 7 days after listing expiration
  • Inactive accounts deleted after 90 days

8. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access, edit, or delete your data
  • Opt out of personalized ads
  • Request data copy or deletion via contact.shafaqat.tech@gmail.com

9. Children’s Privacy

Tijarah is not intended for children under 13. We delete any such data immediately upon discovery.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on Google Cloud servers outside India with adequate protections.

11. Cookies & Similar Technologies

  • Local storage for offline use
  • Analytics and ad identifiers for performance and personalization
  • Session tokens for secure login

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or app changes. Continued use after updates means acceptance.
