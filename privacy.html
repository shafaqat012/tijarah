Privacy Policy for Tijarah

Effective Date: September 2025

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tijarah, a local marketplace platform that connects buyers and sellers in your area. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Tijarah, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This Privacy Policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Google Account Data: Email address and name when signing in with Google
  • Profile Information: Username, ratings, review history
  • Contact Information: Email address for account management
  • GPS Location Data: We collect precise GPS coordinates (fine location) and approximate location (coarse location) when you create or browse listings. This enables accurate location-based search results and helps buyers find products near them. Location is only collected when the app is actively in use, not in the background. You can disable location permissions in your device settings, but this will limit location-based features.

2.2 Content You Create

  • Listings: Product titles, descriptions, prices, categories, photos
  • Messages: Chat conversations with other users
  • Reviews: Ratings and reviews you provide for sellers
  • Search Data: Search queries and filter preferences

2.3 Device & Technical Information

  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, features used
  • Connection Data: Online/offline status
  • Crash Reports: Technical logs for troubleshooting
  • Notification Permissions: Wake lock (to deliver time-sensitive message notifications even when your device is in sleep mode) and vibrate (to alert you of new messages, listing updates, and app notifications)
  • Storage & Media Access: Camera permission for taking product photos directly in the app, and storage access for uploading existing photos from your device gallery

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: App performance metrics, engagement data
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data
  • Google Advertising ID (GAID): We collect your device's Advertising ID on Android 13+ for personalized advertising through Google AdMob. You can reset your advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete advertising ID)
  • Rate Limiting Data: Message frequency tracking to prevent spam

3. What Information Is Public

Publicly Visible to All Users:

  • Username and profile photo
  • Listings (title, description, price, photos, approximate location)
  • Reviews and ratings you've given or received
  • User reputation score

Private Information (Not Publicly Shared):

  • Email address
  • Precise GPS coordinates (only approximate area is shown)
  • Chat messages (visible only to conversation participants)
  • Device information

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Core App Functionality

  • Enable account creation and authentication via Google Sign-In
  • Facilitate buying and selling transactions
  • Process and display listings
  • Enable real-time messaging between buyers and sellers
  • Provide location-based search results and nearby product recommendations

4.2 Safety & Security

  • Prevent spam, fraud, and abuse
  • Moderate content and enforce community safety guidelines
  • Apply rate limiting to prevent message spam
  • Admin review and approval of listings before publication

4.3 Communication

  • Push notifications for new messages, listing updates, and important alerts
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Notify users about account activities and policy updates

4.4 Improvement & Analytics

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and bugs
  • Develop new functionality based on user behavior
  • Performance monitoring and optimization

4.5 Advertising

  • Display relevant ads via Google AdMob and AdMob Mediation partners
  • Personalize advertisements based on your interests and app usage
  • Measure ad performance and engagement

5. Information Sharing & Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate Tijarah:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage, Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob: Primary ad serving and analytics
  • AdMob Mediation Partners: May include Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, and other approved ad networks
  • Google Play Services: Core Android functionality, location services, and authentication

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

5.2 Data Sharing Practices

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Information is shared only as necessary to provide marketplace features (e.g., displaying your listings to other users)
  • Aggregated, anonymized data may be shared for analytics purposes
  • We verify that third-party services comply with applicable privacy standards

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Tijarah, our users, or the public.

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on Google Cloud's secure infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Firestore security rules limit unauthorized access
  • Authentication: Secure Google Sign-In implementation
  • Regular Updates: Continuous security monitoring and vulnerability patching

6.2 Content Moderation

  • Automated content filtering for prohibited items
  • Manual admin review of flagged listings
  • User reporting system for suspicious activity
  • Rate limiting to prevent spam and abuse

Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use Tijarah at your own risk.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

7.1 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account remains active
  • Chat Messages: Stored until manually deleted by users; automatically deleted 7 days after the associated listing expires
  • Listings: Deleted when you remove them or automatically deleted after expiration
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and aggregated after 26 months
  • Inactive Accounts: Automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity

7.2 Account Deletion

  • You can delete your account anytime through app settings (Profile > Settings > Delete Account)
  • Upon deletion, your listings, profile, and personal data are permanently removed
  • Chat messages may persist for other conversation participants until they delete them
  • Some information may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Delete: Remove listings, messages, reviews, and your entire account
  • Control: Manage favorites, notifications, and privacy settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable promotional messages and personalized ads
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions (location, notifications) through device settings

How to Exercise Your Rights:
To access, correct, or delete your data, or to exercise any other privacy rights, email us at contact.shafaqat.tech@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days. You can also manage most data directly through the app settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Tijarah is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us at contact.shafaqat.tech@gmail.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States (Google Cloud servers). By using Tijarah, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

11. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Tijarah uses the following technologies:

  • Local Storage: For offline functionality and temporary data caching
  • Analytics Cookies: To track app usage and performance (Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID for personalized ad delivery
  • Session Tokens: For secure authentication and session management
  • Device Identifiers: To recognize your device and prevent fraud

You can manage some of these settings through your device preferences (Settings > Privacy).

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • The "Last Updated" date will be revised
  • We will notify you via in-app notification or email
  • You may be required to accept the updated policy to continue using the app

Continued use of Tijarah after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
