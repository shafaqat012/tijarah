Tijarah Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your Tijarah account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Tijarah app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your request. This will delete:
    • Your profile information (username, email, profile picture)
    • Your listings
    • Your chat messages
    • Your reviews

Retention notes:

  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months.
  • Chat messages in conversations with other users may persist until those users delete them.

If you encounter any issues, you can also request account deletion via email: contact.shafaqat.tech@gmail.com
